# Records Folder

This folder holds sample record images for practicing source-aware inventory with a file-aware AI assistant.

The five included JPEGs are public National Archives images connected to the von Trapp family's immigration and Maria Augusta von Trapp's naturalization record trail. They are included as a teaching packet, not as a completed proof argument.

## How To Use These Images

Ask your AI assistant to inventory the folder before asking for conclusions:

```text
Please inventory the records folder. Identify each image, describe what kind of record it appears to be, name the likely record trail, and list the next careful extraction steps. Do not infer relationships or conclusions beyond what the images support.
```

Use the results as a starting point for human review. The next steps are to inspect the images, extract assertions, classify information quality assertion by assertion, cite the relevant records, and then decide what evidence the records provide for a focused research question.

## Privacy Note

Codex, Claude Code, and Cowork can read local files, but their standard modes process file contents with cloud models. Keep out documents too sensitive for a cloud service. For highly sensitive work, use a fully local setup or remove sensitive details before analysis.

Protect living persons. Do not add files with addresses, contact details, health information, financial information, or other sensitive details about anyone who could plausibly be alive unless you have deliberately chosen an appropriate privacy-safe workflow.
