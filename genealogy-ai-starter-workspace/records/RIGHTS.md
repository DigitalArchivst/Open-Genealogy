# Record Image Rights

The five bundled JPEGs reproduce U.S. federal immigration, naturalization, and court records held by the National Archives. They are not Steve-authored content and are excluded from the repository's `CC-BY-NC-SA-4.0` license.

## Official Rights Basis

The National Archives identifies materials created by U.S. federal agencies in official duties as U.S. government works that are not eligible for copyright protection in the United States. NARA also cautions that not every item in its holdings is public domain, does not make definitive item-level determinations, and places responsibility for necessary clearances on the user. See NARA's official [Copyright and Permissions](https://www.archives.gov/research/still-pictures/permissions) and [Copyright, Restrictions, and Permissions Notice](https://www.archives.gov/global-pages/privacy.html) pages.

The official [von Trapp article](https://www.archives.gov/publications/prologue/2005/winter/von-trapps-html) and [record citations](https://www.archives.gov/boston/exhibits/von-trapp-citations.html) identify these images as records of the Immigration and Naturalization Service, Record Group 85, and U.S. District Courts, Record Group 21. On that source evidence, the record images are treated here as U.S. government works in the public domain in the United States. This is a source-based status statement, not a license granted by Steve or a definitive item-level ruling by NARA.

## Reuse and Credit

NARA encourages a credit line for documentation. Suggested credit:

> Record image courtesy of the National Archives and Records Administration; von Trapp family immigration or naturalization records; exact file and record-group details as listed above.

Keep the source URL and manifest identifier with each reused image. Verify the official source page and any use restrictions again before publication, especially outside the United States or in contexts involving privacy, publicity, trademark, or other non-copyright rights.
