Prepared for the Open-Genealogy `genealogy-ai-starter-workspace`.

The files are hash-named copies of public National Archives images connected to the von Trapp family's immigration and Maria Augusta von Trapp's naturalization record trail. ## Genealogical Handling Notes

| File | Record type | Genealogical value | Handling boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `record-76aa2644ab.jpg` | Passenger arrival manifest, page 1 | Establishes the arrival-record context and gives a first-pass identity cluster: names, ages, sex, marital status, occupations, nationality or race, places of birth, immigration visa data, and last permanent residence columns. | Digital image of an original source reproduced by the National Archives. Information quality must be assigned assertion by assertion; likely mixed Primary Information, Secondary Information, and Indeterminate Information. Evidence classification depends on a focused research question. |
| `record-04fc6a685c.jpg` | Passenger arrival manifest continuation / immigrant inspector page | Connects passengers to U.S. destination and contact information, prior U.S. visits, ticket/payment clues, admission details, and administrative annotations that may guide later record searches. | Digital image of an original source reproduced by the National Archives. Contact and destination claims are clues until extracted, cited, and correlated. |
| `record-991c5210e8.jpg` | Record of Aliens Held for Special Inquiry | Adds process context that the passenger list alone may not explain: cause of detention or review, board action, admission timing, and cross-references to manifest/group numbers. | Official action entries may be strong process information, but names, relationships, and biographical details still require assertion-level classification. |
| `record-a762640c0f.jpg` | Declaration of Intention | Opens the naturalization trail: name, residence, occupation, birth and marriage claims, spouse reference, children summary, immigration date/vessel/port claim, photograph, signature, and court identifiers for later file retrieval. | The declaration is an original source as a court or agency record, but its information is mixed: some statements reflect the declarant's present action or identity; earlier-life facts may be Secondary Information or Indeterminate Information until the informant basis is evaluated. |
| `record-b16acecff8.jpg` | Petition for Naturalization, page 1 | Correlates and potentially tests the declaration: residence, occupation, birth and marriage claims, spouse status, children summary, immigration date/port/vessel claim, declaration reference, and court process details. | Petition statements require assertion-level classification and correlation with arrival and declaration records. |
